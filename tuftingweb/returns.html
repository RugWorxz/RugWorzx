Returns & Exchanges

We want you to be completely satisfied with your purchase. If for any reason you are not happy with your rug, we offer a simple returns and exchanges process.

Return Policy

You may return your item within 30 days of delivery for a full refund, provided it is in its original condition. Please note that custom-made items are non-refundable unless there is a manufacturing defect.

Exchange Policy

If you wish to exchange your rug for a different size or design, please contact us within 30 days of delivery. The item must be in its original condition, and additional shipping charges may apply.

How to Return or Exchange

To initiate a return or exchange, please contact our customer service team at support@yourrugbusiness.com. We will provide you with detailed instructions and a return shipping label.

Contact Us

If you have any questions about our Returns & Exchanges policy, please contact us at support@yourrugbusiness.com.
