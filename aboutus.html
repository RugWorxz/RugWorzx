Our Story

Founded in 2023, Rug Worxz is dedicated to creating unique and beautiful rugs that add warmth and style to homes around the world. Each piece is carefully crafted with the highest quality materials and attention to detail.

Our mission is to bring exceptional craftsmanship and timeless design to every home, offering a wide range of styles and custom options to suit every taste.

Our Values

We believe in sustainability, quality, and the power of handmade products. Our team is passionate about creating rugs that are not only beautiful but also built to last.
